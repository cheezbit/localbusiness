Find Black-Owned Businesses

Black-Owned Businesses on sites like Etsy will be tagged with a small black fist. Show support for black-owned businesses in a time where they are struggling to get justice.

Find Local Businesses

Due to COVID-19, local businesses have lost profits due to less people going out. Help support them by choosing to shop/eat locally instead of at big corporations. Local businesses in your google results will be tagged with a small house icon.
